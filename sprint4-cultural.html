How understanding the principles of neuroplaticity benefits people

Understanding neuroplaticity will help you on a day to day basis. This here helps you become a better person and apply a postive mindset with anything you do, it helps you handle situations better and take control of your emotions. It helps with overthinking and also anxiety.

Discuss how might engage with the principals of neuroplaticity for your own benefit

Example you go through a situation that is hurtful, every time you think about this situation you are strenghtening this memory in your brain, this means you start thinking about this more. To be able to move on from this situation and learn, you need to be strong and replace these thoughts with something else. Every time this thought comes up in your head you immediatley replace this with something else. Either you start doing something or you think another thought, the more you start doing this your memory of this situation will eventually phase out and you will be able to move on.

What are some of the ways to increase your Neuroplaticity

Become aware of your thoughts and actions, replace these thoughts or actions with something else and do this on repeat.
