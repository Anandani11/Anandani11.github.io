    • What is this?

    Margin, Border and Padding is what you use to create the basic layout of your website, this here creates shapes and sizes around your content making your website look the way it does. Example take a nice juicy Chicken Burger, the paddy inside the burger is your content, the extras so your cheese, sauce and salad is your padding, the hamburger bun is your border box and your Margin is the hamburger box!

    How and When do you use this?

    • Margin - This is what you can use to separate your content. This creates boxes between your paragraphs.
    • Border - This is the outline box around your content, you can use padding to create space between your content making this look like a box.
    • Padding - This is the space between your content and border box, you would use this to make your content look clear and tidy.
